Sahayak — A Digital Help Desk. Personalized for You.

Sahayak is a voice-first, inclusive digital help desk designed to simplify access to essential public services such as healthcare, government welfare, education, employment, legal aid, and emergency support.

Built during Hack For Tomorrow, a 24-hour national-level hackathon held at Model Engineering College, Trikkakara, Ernakulam, on January 11–12, 2026.

Problem Statement

Accessing essential public services is often difficult because:

Information is scattered across multiple platforms
Procedures are complex and difficult to understand
Language barriers limit accessibility
Many users have low digital literacy
Service updates and requirements are not communicated clearly

As a result, people miss opportunities, face delays, and struggle to access services they are eligible for.

Solution

Sahayak acts as a personalized digital help desk that helps users:

Discover relevant public services
Understand eligibility and required documents
Receive step-by-step guidance
Take direct actions like calling helplines or navigating to locations
Access emergency support instantly

The platform is designed with accessibility and simplicity as the primary focus.
